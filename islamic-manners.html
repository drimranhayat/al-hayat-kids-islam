👨‍👩‍👧 Parent & Teacher Note

Gentle guidance
Use this page with encouragement, not pressure. For young children, Islamic manners are best taught through modelling, repetition, short praise, and real-life routines. Read one card, act it out, ask the child to try it once, then celebrate the effort with a star. For Arabic phrases, parents and teachers should guide pronunciation according to their reliable local learning tradition.
