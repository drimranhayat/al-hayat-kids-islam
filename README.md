# Al-Hayat Kids Islam — Official Website

Official website for **Al-Hayat Kids Islam — Official Website** and future app branding **Al-Hayat Kids Islam — Parent-Guided Islamic Learning**.

Urdu name: **الحیات اسلامی اطفال**

## Purpose

A premium, parent-guided Islamic learning website for Muslim children aged 3–8. It introduces the future app, curriculum, age-wise learning path, level-wise learning system, safety promises, privacy policy, and contact pathway.

## First Version Rule

This first version is intentionally **without audio**:

- No audio files
- No sound effects
- No recitation playback
- No voice recording
- No microphone access
- No copyrighted media

Parents and teachers guide pronunciation and memorization.

## Before Publishing

1. Replace `YOUR_DOMAIN_HERE` in HTML canonical tags, Open Graph URLs, `sitemap.xml`, and `robots.txt`.
2. Replace `YOUR_EMAIL_HERE` in contact and privacy pages.
3. Have Arabic Qur’an text, duas, translations, and Prophet story details reviewed by qualified scholars before final publication.
4. Test mobile menu, FAQ accordion, filters, contact form message, and coming-soon Google Play button.
5. Submit `/sitemap.xml` in search consoles after deployment.
