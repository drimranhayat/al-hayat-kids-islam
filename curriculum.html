👨‍👩‍👧 Parent and teacher guidance

Use the curriculum lovingly. The aim is not pressure; the aim is familiarity, habit and love of Islam.

Recommended use: Ages 3–4 should mostly recognize, listen, tap and repeat. Ages 4–5 can answer easy daily-life questions. Ages 5–6 can begin short understanding tasks. Ages 6–7 can do mixed practice. Ages 7–8 can complete stronger review challenges. Keep advanced legal, sectarian or fear-heavy content away from this age range.
