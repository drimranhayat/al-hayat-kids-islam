📜 Terms of Use

Educational use

By using Al-Hayat Kids Islam, parents, teachers and visitors agree to use the website responsibly for child-friendly Islamic learning. These terms are written in simple language for families.

✅ Allowed use

Family and classroom

Home learning

Parents may use pages for daily child learning, revision and practice.

Classroom support

Teachers may use the website as a support resource after reviewing content for their context.

Personal practice

Children may use questions, activities, games and practice boards with adult guidance.

⚠️ Important limits

Please read
  1. The website is not a fatwa service and does not replace qualified Islamic scholars.
  2. The website does not replace parents, teachers, mosque learning or school curriculum.
  3. Parents and teachers should review age suitability before use.
  4. Do not misuse, copy or republish the entire website as your own product.
  5. Do not submit private information about children through contact forms.

🧑‍🏫 Accuracy and corrections

Quality matters

We aim for authentic, respectful and age-appropriate Islamic content. If you notice a mistake, unclear wording, typo or content needing scholarly review, please contact us so it can be corrected.
