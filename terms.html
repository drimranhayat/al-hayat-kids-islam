Terms of Use

Please use this website and future app as parent-guided educational support for young children.

Educational and parent-guided use

The website/app is intended for Islamic educational support under parent or teacher guidance.

Islamic content prepared with care

Content is planned carefully, but parents, teachers, and scholars are welcome to report corrections or improvements.

No formal fatwa

The website/app does not provide formal fatwa, legal rulings, or advanced religious verdicts. Complex religious questions should be referred to qualified scholars.

Pronunciation and memorization

Parents and teachers should support Qur’an, duas, and Arabic pronunciation carefully.

No misuse of content

Do not misuse, misrepresent, or modify Islamic content in a way that creates false religious claims.
