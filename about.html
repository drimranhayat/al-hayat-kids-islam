About the project

What is Al-Hayat Kids Islam?

Al-Hayat Kids Islam is a safe, parent-guided Islamic learning project for Muslim children aged 3–8. It combines early childhood learning principles with simple Islamic content, visual routines, and gentle family guidance.

Why it was created

Many families want a calm, beautiful, trustworthy way to introduce children to Islamic words, duas, short Surahs, manners, Salah basics, Ramadan, Eid, Arabic letters, and Prophets’ stories without ads, unsafe links, or overwhelming content.

This website prepares the official public presence for the app and explains the learning vision clearly for parents, teachers, and future Google Play visitors.

No-audio first version

The first version is visual and parent-guided. It does not include audio, sound effects, recitation playback, voice recording, or microphone access.

Brand position

Website and app, one clear identity

The website is the official trust center. The app will be the parent-guided learning tool.
