👨‍👩‍👧 Parent and teacher notes

Short and practical
Keep it visual Use big letters. Ask the child to point, say, trace and smile. Avoid long lectures.
Do not rush joining For ages 3–5, single-letter recognition is enough. Joining shapes can come later.
Connect to Qur’an gently Say: “Arabic helps us love and read the Qur’an.” Keep the tone warm and respectful.
