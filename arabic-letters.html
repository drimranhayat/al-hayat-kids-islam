Match words

Connect letters to Islamic words gradually and respectfully.

Letter recognition

Children first notice shapes, dots, and directions. Parents can point, name the letter, and connect it with a simple word.

Tracing-style practice

The app can use safe visual tracing cards without collecting handwriting data. Children can trace with finger guidance on paper or screen.

Gradual Alif to Yaa learning

Letters should be grouped gradually. Very young children can begin with a few letters; older children can review more complete sets.
