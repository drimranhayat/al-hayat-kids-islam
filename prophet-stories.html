👨‍👩‍👧 Parent / Teacher Guidance

Safe storytelling
Keep it gentleFor ages 3–8, avoid frightening details. Focus on Allah’s help, good choices, kindness and hope.
Use Qur’anic referencesThese cards give simple references. Read from a reliable children’s Qur’an story book or teacher-approved source for formal lessons.
One moral onlyAfter one story, ask: “What good action can we do today?” Then let the child practise one tiny behaviour.

Note: This page gives simplified child-friendly summaries. For formal teaching, parents and teachers should review Qur’anic verses, authentic Islamic sources and the child’s age level.
