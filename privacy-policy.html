Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Al-Hayat Kids Islam is designed to work as a static educational website with no account system and no child-data collection.

What we collect

The website itself does not ask children to create accounts, submit personal data, use a camera, use a microphone, or share location.

Local progress

Progress such as stars and completed cards may be saved in the browser using local storage. This stays on the device unless the browser is cleared.

Contact email

If an adult emails feedback, the email is handled through the user’s email app/service.

Parent responsibility

Parents and teachers should supervise use and review content before classroom or public institutional use.

Al-Hayat Kids Islam

Safe, parent-guided Islamic learning for children ages 3–8. No child chat, no login, no ads, and no tracking scripts.
