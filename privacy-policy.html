Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains the first website/app vision for parent-guided Islamic learning.

Parent-guided Islamic learning

Al-Hayat Kids Islam is designed for parent-guided Islamic education for children aged 3–8.

No personal data from children

This website version does not collect personal data from children. It does not provide child accounts, login, chat, comments, or upload features.

No ads or tracking

This static version does not include ads, analytics, third-party tracking scripts, advertising identifiers, or external API tracking.

No sensitive permissions

The first app version should not request location, camera, microphone, contacts, or storage permissions. The website does not access these permissions.

Future local progress

If future app progress is used, it should be local-only unless clearly stated otherwise in a future updated policy.
