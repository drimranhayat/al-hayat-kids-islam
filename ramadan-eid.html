Ramadan for children

Children can learn that Ramadan is a blessed month of worship, Qur’an, kindness, patience, family meals, and charity.

Suhoor and Iftar awareness

Young children may not fast, but they can learn the names of suhoor and iftar, help set the table, and make dua with family.

Eid and Dhul Hijjah

Children learn that Eid is a time of gratitude, prayer, family kindness, sharing, and remembering Allah. Older children can also learn basic Hajj awareness.
