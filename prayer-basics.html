👨‍👩‍👧 Parent & Teacher Note

Gentle guidance

This page gives an early-childhood introduction to Salah. Children should learn prayer calmly, step by step, with a trusted parent or teacher. Detailed recitation, school policy and fiqh details should be taught according to the family’s recognised teacher, masjid or curriculum.

Authenticity anchors: the Qur’an commands prayer, teaches preparation for prayer, and reminds believers that prayer is connected with remembering Allah. This page keeps the content simple for ages 3–8.
