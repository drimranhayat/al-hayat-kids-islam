Safety & privacy

Built for parent trust

This static website version is designed to explain the app vision clearly while avoiding ads, tracking scripts, child accounts, unsafe media, and unnecessary permissions.

Parent-guided learning

Children aged 3–8 should use Islamic learning content with parent or teacher support. Adults guide pronunciation, memorization, meaning, and daily-life practice.

Future app progress

If progress tracking is added in the future app, it should remain local-only unless a future policy clearly states otherwise.

Google Play readiness

The site prepares essential trust pages: privacy policy, terms, contact, safety, FAQ, and correction request. Actual app submission should still be checked against the latest Google Play policies before release.
