Gradual learning path

The child moves from recognition to daily-life use. Each task unlocks the next step through simple symbols.

Recognize

See the symbol, picture, Arabic word, or Islamic phrase.

Choose

Answer a short MCQ, tap task, yes/no, or picture question.

Practice

Trace, type, repeat, match, arrange, or complete a mini activity.

Play

Use a game card to review the same idea in a joyful way.

Earn

Get stars and badges, then unlock the next connected task.
