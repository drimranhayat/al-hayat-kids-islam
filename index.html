Age paths

Three simple paths for children

🌱

Age 3–4

Look, point, say simple Islamic words, and build love.

 🌼

Age 5–6

Repeat, match, memorize short duas and early Surahs.

 🌟

Age 7–8

Understand, practice, answer scenarios, and build habits.

Level system

One course can become easy or advanced

👀

Level 1: Look & Learn

Big picture, one Islamic word, parent reads, child repeats.

🧩

Level 2: Practice & Remember

Match, tap, sort, trace, checklist, and gentle review.

💚

Level 3: Use in Life

Scenario questions, good deed challenges, and habit stars.

For busy parents

Designed for children who need clear signs, not long reading

Many parents have very little time. This version makes the first screen action-based: large icons, simple course names, and direct Lesson / Activity / Mini Game buttons. Parent notes are moved lower so the child’s learning path remains clear.
