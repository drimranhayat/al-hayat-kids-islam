5-minute family routine

How the app fits daily Muslim family life

Open one card

Choose a dua, manner, Surah, Arabic letter, or Prophet story card.

Parent reads

The parent explains the meaning gently and guides pronunciation where needed.

Child practices

The child repeats, matches, chooses, traces, or answers one simple scenario.

Use it today

Connect the lesson to eating, sleeping, greeting, sharing, cleaning, or prayer time.

Parent-guided by design

No-audio first version

This first version does not add audio files, sound effects, recitation playback, voice recording, microphone access, or copyrighted media. Parents and teachers guide pronunciation, memorization, and meaning lovingly.

Read parent guidance

Islamic authenticity promise

We do not fabricate Qur’an, Hadith, duas, virtues, rewards, or story details. Exact Arabic text and translations should be reviewed by qualified scholars before publication.
