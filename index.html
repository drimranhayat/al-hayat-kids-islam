What the symbols mean

These guidance connectors make the site easier for a child who cannot read much yet.

🌱 New

A fresh lesson card the child has not done yet.

👆 Tap

Touch the correct answer, button, or choice.

🔁 Again

Practice the same idea once more for confidence.

🔓 Next

This shows the child which activity opens next.
